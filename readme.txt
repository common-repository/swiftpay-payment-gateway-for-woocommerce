== Description ==
Easily accept online bank transfers on your WooCommerce site via the SwiftPay payment
gateway.
As a business owner, you first add a plugin to your checkout page to start accepting payments.
From now on, your clients can finish the purchase by paying with SwiftPay - they select the bank
they have an account with, enter their credentials, select the savings account they want to debit
and that's it! Customer account is debited, your account is credited.

== Benefits ==
Simplified and faster checkout process giving your customers a possibility to complete an instant,
online payment without leaving your website
Access to 5M Filipinos who can pay online using the money stored in their regular savings
accounts, without a need to have credit cards or e-wallet accounts
50% lower payment processing fees compared to other payment gateways available in the
Philippines
SwiftPay accept payments from Mastercard, Visa, American Express and Discover.

== Features ==
1. MULTIPLE PAYMENT METHODS ON YOUR CHECKOUT PAGE
Integrate direct online bank transfers, debit and credit cards and buy now pay later in a matter of minutes.
2. PAYMENT LINKS YOU CAN SEND
Create payment link and share it with your customers via SMS, E-mail, Messenger, Viber or WhatsApp.

== Installation ==
To install the plugin, log in to your WordPress dashboard, go to the Plugins menu and click Add New.
In the search field type "SwiftPay" and click "Search Plugins". Once the SwiftPay plugin shows up, you can click on “Install Now” and then “Activate”.
Next, to configure the plugin go to WooCommerce -> payments and configure the access keys which can be requested from: https://swiftpay.ph/sign-up-now

The installation instruction is also available on: https://swiftpay.ph/enable-swiftpay-on-woocommerce/
